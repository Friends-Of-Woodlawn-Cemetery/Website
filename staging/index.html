Mission Statement

Friends of Woodlawn Cemetery, Wadsworth Ohio seeks to enhance the aesthetic, historic, and educational features within the cemetery; to honor the final resting places for loved ones; and afford a reflective, welcoming space for the entire community.

Sponsor: Midwest Everlasting Memorials

We have been graciously sponsored by Midwest Everlasting Memorials. Their family owned full service memorial company is located in Wadsworth at 1244 High Street (Rt. 94) Their professional staff design, engrave and deliver your family's monument all here in Wadsworth. Their beautiful selection of headstones includes Upgright, Flat Markets, Slant Monuments, Bronze and Custom Headstones. For more information about Midwest Everlasting Memorials, visit their website.
